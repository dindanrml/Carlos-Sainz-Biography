CARLOS SAINZ JR.
El Matador

Background

Carlos Sainz Jr. (Full name: Carlos Sainz Vázquez de Castro Cenamor Rincón Rebollo Virto Moreno de Aranda Don Per Urrielagoiria Pérez del Pulgar) is a Spanish racing driver and now competing in Formula 1 for Scuderia Ferrari. He was born in Madrid, September 1st 1994. Carlos is the son of Carlos Sainz Sr., a two-time World Rally Champion, which has influenced his passion for motorsport. He is known for his dedication, work ethic, and strategic approach to racing.

Early Career

Carlos Sainz began his motorsport career in karting at a young age, showcasing his talent early on. He progressed through various junior formula categories, including Formula Renault and Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0, where he achieved notable success.

Formula 1 Career

Sainz made his Formula 1 debut in 2015 with Scuderia Toro Rosso ( now known as AlphaTauri). His impressive performances led to a move to Renault in 2017, where he continued to develop as a driver. In 2019, he joined McLaren, achieving his first podium finish at the 2020 Italian Grand Prix. In 2021, Sainz signed with Scuderia Ferrari, where he has continued to build on his success, often competing at the front of the grid alongside teammate Charles Leclerc until 2024. And in 2025, he will be joining Williams.
