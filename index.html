Biography

Carlos Sainz VÃ¡zquez de Castro, commonly known as Carlos Sainz Jr., is a Spanish Formula 1 racing driver born on September 1, 1994, in Madrid, Spain. He is the son of two-time World Rally Champion Carlos Sainz Sr. Following in his father's footsteps, Sainz pursued motorsport from a young age, competing in karting before joining junior racing categories, where he quickly made a name for himself. Sainz entered Formula 1 in 2015 with Scuderia Toro Rosso (now AlphaTauri), then moved through Renault and McLaren, where he proved himself as a skillful and consistent driver. In 2021, Sainz joined Ferrari, partnering with Charles Leclerc and achieving several podiums. Known for his adaptability, work ethic, and technical insights, Sainz is respected for his racing acumen and remains a strong contender in F1.
