Who is Carlos Sainz?

Carlos Sainz Jr. (Full name: Carlos Sainz Vázquez de Castro Cenamor Rincón Rebollo Virto Moreno de Aranda Don Per Urrielagoiria Pérez del Pulgar) is a Spanish racing driver and now competing in Formula 1 for Scuderia Ferrari. He was born in Madrid, September 1st 1994. Sainz inspired by his father, Carlos Sainz Sr. and Fernando Alonso and then started to pursuing his career in karting and junior FormulaE.

Career

2015 - Scuderia Toro Rosso
Sainz made his debut with Scuderia Toro Rosso in 2015, and scored 18 points in 15th.

2016 - Scuderia Toro Rosso / Renault Sport F1 Team A second season with Toro Rosso and gained position in 12th with 46 points.

2017 - Scuderia Toro Rosso / Renault Sport F1 Team Sainz's third season with Toro Rosso, then accepted an offer to join Renault for the following season and scored 54 points in 9th.

2018 - Renault Sport F1 Team Sainz scored 53 points in 10th and replaced by Daniel Ricciardo for Renault, ending his association with Red Bull.
