Carlos Sainz Jr.

"El Matador"

Carlos Sainz Jr. is a Spanish racing driver and now competing in Formula 1 for Scuderia Ferrari.

Career

2015 - Scuderia Toro Rosso
2016 - Scuderia Toro Rosso / Renault Sport F1 Team
2018 - Renault Sport F1 Team
2019 - McLaren F1 Team
2020 - McLaren F1 Team
2021 - Scuderia Ferrari Mission Winnow
2022 - Scuderia Ferrari
2023 - Scuderia Ferrari
